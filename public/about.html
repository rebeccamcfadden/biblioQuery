About Us

BiblioQuery is a web-based app for Texas A&M students to search, buy, and sell textbooks. We use price searching APIs to help you find the textbooks you need at a reasonable price. We also make use of multiple book databases to provide cover images, previews, and other rich data to help you narrow your book search. In addition, we provide an avenue for A&M students to post textbooks and sell them to other A&M students. In this way, we can decrease book waste and use the Aggie Network to reduce textbook costs for students.
